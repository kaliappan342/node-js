Birthday Theme: Retro Arcade

Gifts: Old-school gaming consoles, custom t-shirts with pixel art.

Design: Neon lights, black tablecloths, Pac-Man decorations. Use bright, contrasting colors.

Wedding Anniversary Idea: Stargazing Getaway

Gifts: Personalized star map (showing the sky on the wedding night), high-quality telescope, weekend trip cabin.

Design: Deep blue/silver color palette, fairy lights (twinkle effect), minimalist decor for a focus on the outdoors.

Team Building Event: Gourmet Cooking Class

Gifts: Branded apron set, gourmet spice collection, professional chef knife.

Design: Rustic kitchen setting, individual cooking stations, use of fresh herbs and natural wood accents.

Graduation Party: Future Focus

Gifts: High-end laptop bag, professional portfolio, subscriptions to relevant industry journals.

Design: Modern and clean lines, metallic accents (gold/silver), photo booth with career props.
